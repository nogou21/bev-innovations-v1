Test Runs for New Products

We offer test runs for new customers and flavors with the following parameters:

- Batch Size:500-gallon batch per SKU.

- Production Output:240 aluminum bottles (10 cases), with 9 cases for the customer and 1 case retained as a sample.

- Cost Inclusions:Batch, water, CO2or, production, can and end Kraft tray, shrink wrap, and label. Additional ingredients may incur extra charges.

- Testing:Includes 3rd party plate and pathogen testing with a Certificate of Analysis provided.
